About Me

The story behind the pole, the passion, and the purpose.

Hey, I'm Ulkar

Welcome to my corner of the pole world! I'm a pole dancer, competitor, and instructor based in Toulouse, France. My journey with aerials began in 2020 and has since become my greatest passion.

Whether I'm training for competitions, teaching private classes, or creating new choreography, pole dance allows me to express myself and push my limits every day. I love connecting with the pole community and helping others discover the joy and strength that comes with this art form.

On this site, I share my experiences, tips, and resources for pole dancers at every level. Feel free to reach out — I'd love to connect!

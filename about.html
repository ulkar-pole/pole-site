About Me

The story behind the pole, the passion, and the purpose.

🩰

Hey, I'm Ulkar

Welcome to my corner of the pole world. I'm a pole dancer, instructor, and forever student of movement. This blog is where I share everything I've learned — from technique breakdowns to choreography ideas, training tips to competition prep.

My pole journey started [your year], and since then it's become so much more than a hobby. It's become a way to express creativity, build strength, connect with an incredible community, and constantly challenge myself to grow.

I believe in breaking down complex moves into understandable progressions, in celebrating all styles of pole, and in making pole knowledge accessible to everyone — whether you're just starting out or working toward competition.

Through this blog, I hope to contribute to the pole community and help others on their own journeys. Feel free to reach out if you have questions, requests, or just want to connect!
